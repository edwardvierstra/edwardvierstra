$0 Budget News - 3/15/25

Minecraft Bedrock Edition is sadly discontinued for MacOS, starting this month. Due to the version of the game being made primarily for Windows in C++, Mojang will not give access to MacOS users, with the only way being the unofficial Linux launcher (https://mcpelauncher.readthedocs.io/en/latest/getting_started/index.html).
